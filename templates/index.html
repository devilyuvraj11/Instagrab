How to download video from Instagram?

Follow these steps to save any public Instagram reel, story, photo or carousel.

1

Copy the link

On Instagram, tap the Share icon and choose Copy link.

2

Paste the link here

Return to insta-grab and paste the copied link into the box above.

3

Select content type

Choose Photo, Reel, Stories or Carousel to match your link.

4

Click Download

Hit the Download button. We’ll fetch the media securely from our server.

5

Preview your content

Check the preview to confirm it’s the correct Instagram post or reel.

6

Save to your device

Save the file directly to your phone or computer for offline viewing.
