How to download video from Instagram?

Follow these simple steps to save any public Instagram Reel, Post, Story or Carousel to your device.

1

Copy the link

Tap the Share icon (⋯) and choose Copy link to copy the post URL to your clipboard.

2

Paste the link here

Return to InstaGrab, paste the copied URL into the link box at the top of the page.

3

Select content type

Tap the matching tab: Photo, Reel, Stories, or Carousel so we can optimize the download.

4

Click “Download”

Press the Download button. We will fetch the media using a secure server connection.

5

Preview your video

Once the preview appears, quickly check that it is the correct Instagram content.

6

Save to your device

Click Download again to save the video file directly to your phone or computer.

Important: Private or login-only posts can only be downloaded if you have access to view them.
Example links: https://www.instagram.com/reel/XXXXXXXXXXX/
Tip: To download private posts add a cookies.txt (Netscape format) to project root. Only use this for content you are allowed to download.
